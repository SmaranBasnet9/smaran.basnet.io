Attentive Unveils Next Generation of Agentic AI Marketing

June 15, 2026 Summarized by Smaran

Attentive has rolled out a new generation of agentic AI tools — Brand Voice 2.0, a Reporting Agent, Predictive Analytics, and AI Campaigns — aimed at letting marketing teams automate more of the strategy-to-execution loop.

Brand Voice 2.0 is built to keep generated copy consistent with a brand's tone across channels, rather than producing the generic output that's made a lot of teams wary of AI-written campaigns. The Reporting Agent and Predictive Analytics push further into territory that used to require a dedicated analyst: surfacing which campaigns are underperforming and forecasting where budget should move next, in plain language rather than a dashboard someone has to interpret.

The company says more than $6B in revenue was driven through its platform in Q1 2026 alone — a number worth treating as a vendor's own claim rather than independently verified, but still a useful signal of how fast agentic workflows are being adopted in retail marketing specifically.

The practical takeaway: this is less about a single flashy feature and more about how much of the campaign pipeline — copy, reporting, budget shifts — is starting to run with an AI agent doing the first pass, and a human reviewing rather than building from scratch.
